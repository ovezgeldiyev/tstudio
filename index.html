Frequently Asked Questions

It is a long established fact that a reader will be distracted by the readable content of a page when looking at its layout.

Who are we?

TStudio, founded by TurboSaif, a creative force in the world of interior and exterior design for popular European FiveM servers. In 2023, TurboSaif joined forces with Hex-Scripts, a renowned scripting service for FiveM, taking the art of mapping to new heights on their platform. With TStudio, TurboSaif embarks on a fresh solo journey, dedicated to quality and collaboration.

Can I customize my interiors?

Absolutely! Our textures are conveniently stored in a texture dictionary (*.ytd), making it a breeze for you to personalize them according to your unique requirements.

Are your files protected?

Yes, all our custom files are securely encrypted by cfx to preserve the intellectual property of our 3D artists. However, we don't lock files that belong to the original GTA (e.g., exterior buildings).

I have an issue, can I get assistance?

Certainly! Visit our Discord server, where you'll find comprehensive support. Please be patient while waiting for our response, as we may be in different time zones.

I'm experiencing compatibility issues with another creator's MLO/Map, what can I do?

If you encounter compatibility challenges, head over to our Discord server and submit a compatibility request. We'll address it based on our availability and feasibility.

How will my subscription renew?

Your subscription will automatically renew 30 days after the initial subscription date. Please note that forgotten cancellations are not eligible for refunds.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you have the freedom to cancel your subscription at any time, with no obligations. Please be aware that your keymaster assets will be instantly revoked, and you'll need to re-subscribe to keep them active.
